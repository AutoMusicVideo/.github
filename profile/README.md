## What is AutoMV
AutoMV is a project to make workflow for making your local Music Video from other source like Youtube, and adding your lossless music.

## How it works?
0. You need to get lossless audio beforehand and verify.
1. A workflow will grab video and use your downloaded lossless music from playform like Qobuz, Apple Music and more
2. It will sync based on our config while keeping your audio intact.
3. Enjoy!
