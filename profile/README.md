## What is AutoMV?
AutoMV is a project with workflow for making your local MV from other source like Youtube, and adding your lossless music.

In simplest way: You provide correct audio, we provide source, you run, you got <sub><sup>unofficial</sup></sub> MV!

## How it works?
0. You need to get lossless audio beforehand and verify.
1. A workflow will grab video and use your downloaded lossless music from playform like Qobuz, Apple Music and more
2. It will sync based on our config while keeping your audio intact.
3. Enjoy!

## Legality
Since we only provide public source and you have to source music for your own, this project is legal as long as we and you don't distribute anywhere unless the author allowed to do so.
